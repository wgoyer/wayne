Heroku! The geocities of this decade?

Back in nineteen diggity 5 (or 1995) GeoCities was a thing. This was the place for anyone who had downloaded a handful of gifs to show the world those gifs. All that was required was knowledge of the marquee HTML tag and FTP. Yahoo! closed GeoCities in 2009, and the internet evolved from dancing jesus to selfies and cat pictures. The future is here!

Some people may still desire to have their own website. Afterall, what's the point of owning your many stupid domain names if they don't go anywhere? So here we are. This guide will walk you through using many of the cool hip new technologies to create a free web page connected to your very own database.
